Emergency & Support Helplines

Access government helplines for immediate assistance on farming issues, schemes, and emergencies

Krishi Rakshak Portal

For reporting crop loss, agricultural grievances, and seeking immediate assistance during natural calamities or pest attacks.

Helpline: 14447

Purpose: Crop loss reporting & grievances

Availability: 24x7

Toll Free

PM-KISAN Helpline

For all queries related to PM-KISAN Samman Nidhi scheme including registration, payment status, corrections in details, and beneficiary verification.

Helpline 1: 155261

Helpline 2: 011-24300606

Purpose: PM-KISAN scheme queries

Scheme Support

PM Fasal Bima Yojana (Crop Insurance)

Hon. Minister Office - Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

Department: Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

📌 Important Information

  • All helpline numbers are toll-free and available for farmers across India
  • Keep your Aadhaar number, land records, and scheme enrollment details handy when calling
  • For best results, call during working hours (10 AM - 6 PM) on weekdays
  • You can communicate in Hindi, English, or your regional language
  • Note down the complaint/reference number provided for follow-up
