Government Schemes for Farmers

Comprehensive guide to agricultural schemes and subsidies available in India

Major Agricultural Schemes

Stay updated with the latest government schemes and subsidies to boost your farming income

PM-KISAN Samman Nidhi

Direct income support to all landholding farmer families in the country to supplement their financial needs for agriculture and allied activities.

Benefit: ₹6,000 per year in 3 installments

Eligibility: All landholding farmers

Apply at: pmkisan.gov.in

₹6,000 Annual

PM Fasal Bima Yojana

Comprehensive crop insurance scheme providing financial support to farmers in case of crop loss due to natural calamities, pests, and diseases.

Premium: 2% for Kharif, 1.5% for Rabi

Coverage: All crops and risks

Apply at: pmfby.gov.in

Crop Insurance

Soil Health Card Scheme

Free soil testing and nutrient-based fertilizer recommendations to improve soil health and reduce input costs for farmers.

Benefit: Free soil testing every 3 years

Covers: 12 soil parameters

Apply at: Local agriculture office

Free Testing

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Flexible credit facility for farmers to meet their short-term credit requirements including crop production, post-harvest expenses, and marketing.

Interest Rate: 7% (4% with subsidy)

Limit: Based on land holding

Apply at: Any bank branch

Low Interest

PM-KUSUM Yojana

Solar agriculture scheme providing financial support for solar pumps, grid-connected solar plants, and solar power plants on agricultural land.

Subsidy: Up to 60% of cost

Covers: Solar pumps and plants

Apply at: State electricity boards

Solar Power

e-NAM (National Agriculture Market)

Online marketing platform for agricultural commodities allowing farmers to sell their produce directly to buyers at better prices.

Benefit: Better price discovery

Access: 1000+ mandis

Apply at: enam.gov.in

Online Market

Micro Irrigation Scheme

Financial assistance for installation of drip and sprinkler irrigation systems to improve water use efficiency and increase productivity.

Subsidy: 55% for general farmers

Additional: 20% for SC/ST farmers

Apply at: District agriculture office

Water Saving

Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanization

Financial assistance for purchase of agricultural machinery and equipment to promote farm mechanization and reduce drudgery.

Subsidy: 40-50% of cost

Covers: Tractors, harvesters, etc.

Apply at: District collector office

Equipment Subsidy

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana

Promotes organic farming by providing financial assistance for organic inputs, certification, and marketing support for organic produce.

Support: ₹50,000 per hectare

Duration: 3 years

Apply at: Agriculture department

Organic Support

National Livestock Mission

Comprehensive scheme for development of livestock sector providing support for animal healthcare, breeding, and dairy development.

Support: 50% of project cost

Covers: Cattle, buffalo, goat farming

Apply at: Animal husbandry department

Livestock Support

Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture

Comprehensive development of horticulture sector covering fruits, vegetables, flowers, spices, and plantation crops.

Support: Up to 40% of cost

Covers: All horticultural activities

Apply at: Horticulture department

Horticulture

Gramin Bhandaran Yojana

Financial assistance for construction of rural godowns and storage infrastructure to reduce post-harvest losses and improve farmers' income.

Subsidy: 25% of project cost

Capacity: 100-30,000 tonnes

Apply at: NABARD offices

Storage Support

How to Apply for Government Schemes

1

Check Eligibility

Review scheme requirements and ensure you meet all eligibility criteria before applying.

2

Gather Documents

Collect required documents like Aadhaar, land records, bank details, and farming certificates.

3

Submit Application

Apply online through respective portals or visit nearest Common Service Center (CSC).

4

Track Status

Monitor your application status online and follow up for any additional requirements.
